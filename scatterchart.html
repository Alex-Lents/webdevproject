Scatter Chart

Scatter charts are best at comparing two different quantitative variables, or variables whose magnitude can be measured with numbers. They work especially well when showing the frequency of data values around certain areas, as the points will be more dense, a phenomenon that is easily noticeable. An exception to this is if one of the variables is time, in which case there is an even better visualization for.
