Bar Chart

Bar charts are best for representing categorical data, or data which can be separated into groups. Humans are very good at using length to distinguish objects from each other, and bar charts make great use of this by having longer bars mean larger values for the category. Compared to column charts, bar charts project horizontally rather than vertically.
